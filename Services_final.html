Professional care for your pet

VetPro aims to provide a high quality ,friendly and community-based veterinary service to our beloved pet owners.

Services Offered
Consultation

Receive expert advice and guidance from our experienced veterinarians to address any concerns or questions about your pet's health and well-being.

Vaccination

Protect your pet from harmful diseases with our comprehensive vaccination services, tailored to your pet's specific needs and lifestyle.

Surgeries

Trust our skilled veterinary surgeons to perform a range of surgical procedures with precision and care, ensuring the best possible outcomes for your pet.

Deworming

Protect your pet from parasitic infections with our deworming treatments, designed to eliminate worms and prevent reinfestation, promoting your pet's overall health.

Laboratory

Access advanced diagnostic testing and analysis through our state-of-the-art laboratory, helping to identify and treat medical conditions quickly and accurately.

Grooming

Treat your pet to a pampering session with our grooming services, including baths, haircuts, nail trims, and more, to keep them looking and feeling their best.

Pet Boarding

Ensure peace of mind while you're away with our safe and comfortable pet boarding facilities, where your pet will receive personalized care and attention.

Dental Care

Maintain your pet's oral health with our professional dental care services, including cleanings, examinations, and treatment for dental issues.
